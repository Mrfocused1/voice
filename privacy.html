Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Culture Voices ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our voice cloning service.

Your Privacy Matters

Voice data is highly sensitive biometric information. We employ industry-leading security measures to protect your data and give you full control over your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Biometric Information (Voice Data)

When you use our voice cloning service, we collect:

  • Audio recordings: Voice samples you upload or record (minimum 30 seconds)
  • Voice characteristics: Acoustic features extracted from your voice for cloning purposes
  • Voice models: AI-generated representations of your voice
  • Transcriptions: Text transcriptions of your audio (auto-generated or user-provided)
  • Generated audio: Speech content created using your cloned voice

Legal Notice (US): Under the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA) and similar laws, we must obtain your explicit consent before collecting biometric data. By using our Service, you consent to our collection and use of your voice data as described in this policy.

2.2 Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Account credentials (encrypted passwords)
  • Creator type (TikTok creator, YouTuber, etc.)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

2.3 Usage Information

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • IP address and location data
  • Service usage statistics (features used, generation frequency)
  • Performance and error logs

2.4 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and maintain session security.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Voice Data

  • Create and maintain your voice cloning model
  • Generate synthetic speech in your voice
  • Improve voice quality and accuracy
  • Detect and prevent misuse or fraudulent activity

Account Information

  • Provide and maintain the Service
  • Authenticate your identity and prevent unauthorized access
  • Communicate with you about updates, features, or issues
  • Process payments and subscriptions

Usage Information

  • Analyze and improve Service performance
  • Personalize your experience
  • Detect technical issues and security threats
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. AI Model Training

Your Voice, Your Control

We do NOT use your individual voice data to train general AI models or create voices for other users. Your voice model is exclusively yours.

However, we may use aggregated, anonymized data (not identifiable to you) to:

  • Improve overall system performance
  • Enhance voice quality algorithms
  • Develop new features

You can opt out of contributing anonymized data by contacting us at privacy@culturevoices.com.

6. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted to and from our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
  • Storage Security: Voice data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict authentication and authorization protocols
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Monitoring: 24/7 system monitoring for unauthorized access
  • Data Isolation: Your voice model is isolated and not accessible to other users

While we employ industry-standard security, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Voice Data: Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after account closure (unless you request immediate deletion)
  • Generated Audio: Stored for 30 days after generation, then automatically deleted (you can delete immediately)
  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active and for 1 year after closure for legal compliance
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 2 years for security and analytics purposes
